2020 YOUNG FLY ACTION

I was participated in the Youth Fly Action held by Taiwan Ministry of Education.Youth Fly Action is a project in order to encourage young people to spontaneously connect with international resources and improve the current situation we want to change.Throughout the contest, my team,Aim High gained a total of 70,000nt fund for our project. Aim High, aims to provide the introduction of most of the university subjects to high school students and share our experiences in academic study with them. Our team consists of 4 college students, which share the same high school.Throughout the advance process of higher education, we confronted a variety of obstacles. We hope to weave our experience and vision into our project, in order to structuralize the information about higher education. We look forward to convert these resources into a robust support for Taiwan’s highschool students.

2019 KU&NTU PROGRAM

I was admitted to the exchange program of Kobe University in 2019. This program is mainly aimed at students from National Taiwan University and Kobe University, providing a platform for communication,progressing through a semester. Through the process, we held weekly meeting and exchange ideas for different content and propose solutions to the problems. The members came from different backgrounds. For example, one of my group members majors in art. After 15 weeks of meetings, Kobe University originally planned to come to Taiwan for exchanges and a final presentation. Unfortunately, it was cancelled due to the pandemic. Through this project, I am more aware to multi-culture thinking , and therefore being more passionate to exchange programs.
